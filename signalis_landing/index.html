Characters

Replika Units:

ADLR (Adler)

ADLR or Adler, are Generation 5 High Command Specialist Replikas. Adlers are Protektor Führungskommando Administrator units. Adler means "Eagle" in German.

ARAR (Ara)

ADLR or Ara, are Generation 2 Low-Cost General Purpose Replikas used in Repair, Maintenance, Construction and Production of Industrial goods. The bird that they are referenced as is the Macaw.

EULR (Eule)

EULR or Eule, are Generation 4 Low-Cost General Purpose Replikas. These units are the standard multi-purpose unit of the Eusan Nation deployed in domestic tasks. Eule means "Owl" in German.

FKLR (Falke)

FKLR or Falke, are Generation 6 High-Tech Bioresonance Command Replikas. These Replikas are used in every AEON facility across the Eusan Nation as the commander of the Protektor security forces of these facilities. Falke means "Falcon" in German.

KLBR (Kolibri)

KLBR or Kolibri, are Generation 6 High-Tech Bioresonance Specialist Replikas. These Units are used as Commando Control Units, using their Bio-resonance to increase squad cohesion as well as improve the mental stability of all units in a squad. Their role is described as being an 'Elite Protektor'. Kolibri means "Hummingbird" in German.

LSTR (Elster)

LSTR or Elster, are Generation 5 Kosmo-Pioneer Specialist Replikas designed to assist orbital missions or Recon Missions. Elster means Magpie in German.

MNHR (Mynah)

MNHR or Mynah units are Generation 3 Industrial Specialist Replikas. These units are deployed in hazardous work environments across the Eusan Nation, including in the Sierpinski mining operation. Their name is a reference to mynas, a family of songbirds native to southern Asia. It’s also likely a form of wordplay - myna is a homophone for miner.

STAR (Starling)

STAR or Star, are Generation 4 Low-Cost Combat Replikas. These units are Protektor Security Technician Guards. Starlings are a type of bird noted for their social behavior and tendency to swarm.

STCR (Storch)

STCR or Storch, are Generation 5 Combat Lead Replikas. These units are deployed as overwatch and coordination of STARs. Storch means "Stork" in German, likely a reference to their tall build.

Gestalts:

Ariane Yeong

Ariane Yeong is a Gestalt and a pilot of the Penrose 512. According to her medical record, she was born on Leng and seems to have lived most of her life prior to becoming a Penrose pilot on Rotfront.

Isolde Itou

Isolde "Isa" Itou is one of the main characters in SIGNALIS. She is referred to as "Isa" throughout the game.

Alina Seo

Alina Seo was a Gestalt (non-replika human) worker at Sierpinski S-23 as well as a Veteran of the Vinetan War.

Erika Itou

Erika Itou is the twin sister of Isa Itou, and the subject of the formers search.
